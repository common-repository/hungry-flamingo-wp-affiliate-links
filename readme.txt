Get advanced functionality for affiliate links and finally master affiliate-based monetization of your blog or website. Add beautiful and recognizable affiliate links. Embedded in your content and in blocks. 

== Description ==

This plugin helps you with monetizing your website via affiliate programs. Automatically convert hyperlinks across your website into beautiful and effective affiliate ads.

== Features ==


= Automatic affiliate ad generation =

Enable this feature to automatically filter your hyperlinks across your posts. Add an icon and let the plugin convert your hyperlinks into beautiful and effective embedded affiliate ads.

= Affiliate Ad Block =

Utilize the Gutenberg block to place a beautiful affiliate ad. No need to embed third-party scripts and compromise the privacy of your visitors. The ad is rendered by your server.

= Compatible with all affiliate programs =

This plugin is not restricted to any affiliate program. Simply add the affiliate's base url and let the plugin convert hyperlinks to this target into effective ads.

= Upload your own affiliate icons =

You can upload your own icon per affiliate to make your ads stand out and look even better.
